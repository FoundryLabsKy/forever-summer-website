Built on a feeling everyone remembers.

The story

Some summers shouldn't end.

Opened 2019, on the Paseo.

Forever Summer opened at Camana Bay in March 2019, the fourth boutique from Catherine and Ian Dawson-James — the family behind Sand Angels, Little Angels and Activ Angels. Catherine has spent more than 25 years in Cayman retail fashion, and this shop is her most personal idea:

“that nostalgic feeling of long summer days and nights filled with fun and freedom”

— kept alive on a rail, at prices that let you say yes more than once.
