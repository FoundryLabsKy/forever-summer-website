# AUDIT — Forever Summer (Camana Bay, Grand Cayman)

Research date: 15 July 2026. Method: public web research (search-index snapshots of the
business's own site, landlord profiles, directories, review aggregators, local press).
Direct page fetches were blocked by this environment's network policy, so facts come from
search-index extracts of the cited URLs; anything not directly verifiable is marked
`UNCONFIRMED`.

## A0. Disambiguation — which Forever Summer?

**Audited entity:** Forever Summer, the **women's lifestyle boutique on The Paseo
(45 Market St), Camana Bay, Grand Cayman**, owned by **Catherine Dawson-James** (with Ian
Dawson-James) — sister store of Sand Angels, Little Angels and Activ Angels.

How identity was confirmed: the Camana Bay opening announcement, the Dart.ky spotlight,
Cayman Resident profile and the business's own domain (foreversummer.com) all describe the
same boutique with the same phone (345-949-4142), email (info@foreversummer.com) and
Paseo location, and all tie ownership to the Dawson-Jameses. This is **not** the UK
"Forever Summer" beachwear brand (facebook.com/foreversummeruk), not the tea brand, and
not any US boutique of the same name. Every fact below anchors to the Camana Bay store.

## A1. Company profile

- **Trade name:** Forever Summer (corporate group: the Dawson-Jameses' Sand Angels Inc.
  family of boutiques — grouping `UNCONFIRMED` at the registry level).
- **What it is:** a women's lifestyle boutique — "a chic and fun mix of affordable
  fashion," trend-led, aimed at women incl. teens and young adults, with **new styles
  arriving weekly in strictly limited quantities** ([Camana Bay profile](https://www.camanabay.com/grand-cayman-shops-restaurants/forever-summer/), [foreversummer.com](https://www.foreversummer.com/))
- **Opened:** announced 27 March 2019 as Camana Bay's newest shop ([opening story](https://www.camanabay.com/stories/camana-bay-welcomes-newest-shop-forever-summer/))
- **Owners:** Catherine Dawson-James (25+ years of retail fashion experience in Cayman)
  and Ian Dawson-James ([Dart.ky spotlight](https://www.dart.ky/news-and-media/spotlight-on-forever-summer/))
- **Founding concept, in the owner's words:** capture "that nostalgic feeling of long
  summer days and nights filled with fun and freedom" ([Dart.ky](https://www.dart.ky/news-and-media/spotlight-on-forever-summer/))
- **Sister boutiques (same owners, all Camana Bay):** Sand Angels (designer swim),
  Little Angels (kids), Activ Angels (active), Riviera (resort lifestyle).
- **Locations:** one store, on the Paseo — described variously as "next to Starbucks,"
  "across from Island Jewellers," "next door to Bang & Olufsen."

## A7. Market context

- **Demand cycle:** Cayman high season early winter→spring with record 2026 arrivals
  (49,075 stayover in Feb; best-ever March/April), traditionally slower Jul–Sept
  ([Cayman News Service](https://caymannewsservice.com/2026/05/tourism-boom-continues-with-record-breaking-april/), [Cayman Compass](https://www.caymancompass.com/2024/11/19/stayover-tourism-numbers-show-recent-decline/), [Tourism Analytics](https://tourismanalytics.com/caymanislands-statistics.html))
- **Customer mix:** unlike swim-led Sand Angels, Forever Summer's affordable positioning
  serves **residents (incl. teens/young adults) year-round** plus visitors; Camana Bay
  town centre (40+ locally owned shops, cinema, events) drives repeat local footfall
  ([Camana Bay](https://www.camanabay.com/stories/camana-bay-welcomes-newest-shop-forever-summer/))
- **Logistics/regulatory:** import-duty economy; "affordable" is a genuine differentiator
  in a high-cost retail market; KYD/USD dual currency. (Duty specifics `UNCONFIRMED` —
  general Cayman retail context.)
