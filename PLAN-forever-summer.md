# PLAN — Forever Summer website

Built from `AUDIT-forever-summer.md`. Deliverable: a self-contained, hash-routed,
single-file marketing site (`index.html`), matching this repo's convention. Must feel
**nothing like** the Sand Angels site: youthful, sunny, attainable vs. gallery-luxury.

## Sitemap

- `#/home` — hero (nostalgic endless-summer, affordable) → "New this week" drop concept →
  what we stock → FS Girls → visit strip → Instagram bridge → CTA
- `#/new` — "New this week": explains the weekly limited-drop model ("when it's gone,
  it's gone"), placeholder drop grid clearly labeled for owner photos, IG link
- `#/shop` — category tour per A3: dresses & apparel, accessories, beauty, gifts
- `#/fs-girls` — the teen/young-adult line (kept from current site)
- `#/visit` — canonical NAP (45 Market St, The Paseo, Camana Bay), wayfinding ("on the
  Paseo, next to Starbucks"), hours (confirm flag), call/email actions
- `#/about` — Catherine's story (25+ yrs Cayman fashion retail), the nostalgia concept in
  her own sourced words, sister-boutique family cross-links

## Key features

- **The drop, productized:** the differentiator from A6 — weekly limited arrivals — is
  the hero mechanic. "First look" email signup (mailto-compose, no backend, honest about
  how it works) + prominent Instagram bridge (@foreversummercayman is where drops live).
- **Contact prominence:** sticky CTA; `tel:+13459494142`; `mailto:info@foreversummer.com`.
- **Service promise:** friendly fitting-room promise (answers the review complaint);
  holds policy marked for owner confirmation.
- **Seasonality:** "it's always summer here" angle turns Cayman's year-round warmth into
  brand voice; resident-first copy with visitor secondary.

## Design tokens (derived from A5 concept; owner logo to confirm)

- **Color (60-30-10):** dominant sun-bleached warm white (`#FFFBF2`→`#1C1510` tinted
  scale); secondary golden-hour amber scale (50–900 around `#E8890C`); **one** accent:
  hibiscus pink-red `#E23D5B`-family, CTAs only; dusk-blue `#274060` as deep neutral
  anchor; semantic colors tinted warm.
- **Type:** display **Fraunces** (soft-serif with 70s-postcard warmth, fits nostalgia) +
  body/UI **Karla** (friendly grotesque; not Inter). Scale 1.28 from 16px, rem.
- **Spacing:** 8px base; generous.
- **Radius:** rounded-friendly 10–16px with pill CTAs (playful personality; deliberately
  opposite Sand Angels' near-sharp).
- **Elevation:** soft layered warm shadows, low elevation count.
- **Signature authored detail:** a retro "endless sun" SVG — striped sunset arcs — as the
  hero art and a repeating wave-stripe divider; gentle marquee of drop-week phrases;
  reduced-motion respected.
- Grayscale-first composition; amber/hibiscus applied last.
